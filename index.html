What Physicians Ask Before Hiring a Social Media Agency

I don't think social media works for doctors.
The data says otherwise. The vast majority of patients research their doctors online before booking. Your competitors are already there. The question isn't whether social media works for physicians — it's whether patients can find YOU when they search.

I tried posting before and it didn't do anything.
Posting randomly without a strategy doesn't work — that's like a patient self-diagnosing on WebMD vs. seeing a specialist. We use data-driven strategies specific to your specialty, with content formats proven to drive engagement and patient inquiries.

How much time will this take from me?
With our Done-For-You packages, less than 30 minutes per month. We send you content for quick approval via text or email — a thumbs up is all we need. You focus on patients, we focus on your presence.

What about HIPAA? I can't post patient info.
Absolutely right — and we'd never ask you to. Every piece of content goes through our HIPAA-aware review process. We focus on educational content, your expertise, behind-the-scenes practice culture, and properly consented patient stories. Your patients' privacy is sacred.

Why can't I just hire someone cheap on Fiverr?
Would you send a patient to a discount surgeon? Generic social media managers don't understand medical marketing, HIPAA compliance, or what converts healthcare consumers. We specialize in physician social media — the strategies, the compliance, and the content that builds real authority in your market.

How long until I see results?
Most physicians see measurable engagement growth within 30 days and patient inquiries from social media within 60–90 days. With paid ads, results can come even faster. We track everything and report monthly so you always know your ROI.

What if I'm not happy with the results?
We offer a 90-day growth guarantee. If you don't see measurable engagement growth within 90 days, we'll work for free until you do. We track everything transparently and report monthly, so you'll always know exactly what's working.

Do you work with multiple physicians in the same area?
We limit the number of physicians we work with per specialty per market area. This ensures your content strategy and messaging stays unique to your practice, and you're never competing with another SocialRx client for the same patients.

Which social media platforms do you manage?
We manage Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube, and X (Twitter). Our Growth Accelerator and Authority Builder plans cover 5+ platforms. We'll recommend the best mix based on your specialty and where your ideal patients spend their time.

Can I cancel at any time?
